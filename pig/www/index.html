Pig

Rules

Each player rolls the dice until they get a 1 or decide to hold, which adds the points in the bucket to their score. The first player to 100 wins.

  • If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and pass the dice to the other player.
  • If the player rolls anything besides a 1, the points go into the bucket.
  • At that point, the player can hold, which adds the points in the bucket to their score, or roll again.
  • The bucket gets bigger with each roll, but if the player rolls a 1, they lose everything in the bucket and pass the dice.
